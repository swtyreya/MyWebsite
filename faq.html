Frequently Asked Question


1. How can I purchase order?

• Create an account on our website
• If you experience any difficulties on purchasing orders on our website, you can send as a message thru our live chat for assistance.
• Or you can also get in touch with us via our Facebook Account for further assistance
• Still we encourage all our shoppers to purchase with their own account


2. How long do you deliver orders?

• For COD within Metro Manila only, 1 to 2 working days
• For provincial areas, via JRS, 3 to 4 working days
• Please take note that Sunday and holidays are not counted.


3. How can I Track my Order?

• Simply log-in your KILY Account and click MY ORDER from there you can check the status of your order.
• For COD deliveries within metro manila, you can send us a message thru our website’s chat box, contact details provided or thru our Facebook Account for further assistance.
• For JRS courier you can check your order with the tracking number provided to you by our CSR.
• Or you can send us a message thru our website’s chat box, contact details provided or thru our Facebook Account for further assistance.


4. Do you offer Cash on Deliver (COD)?

• Yes
• COD is applicable only within Metro Manila you will be you QUICK TIME Courier
• We do not cater COD in provincial areas.


5. Do you offer FREE Shipping?

• No, all shipping cost should be shouldered by customers


6. How much is the shipping rates?

• Shipping rates are computed based on the dimension and weight of your order. Please refer to our shipping rate policy for your reference.


7. What are your Payment Methods?

• You can settle your order thru our Bank Accounts: BDO, Metrobank and BPI FAMILY.
• Payment Centers: Palawan Express, Cebuana and LBC only.
• For COD orders within metro manila you can pay directly to our private rider.
• For PICK UP at Juan Luna, you can settle your order direct to our stall.
• For PICK UP at Maginhawa Store, can settle thru bank accounts and remittance centers.


8. Can I cancel my Order?

• Yes, But you need to contact our customer service representatives ahead of time via website’s chat-box, contact details provided or via our Facebook Account for further assistance.


9. How to return my order?

• Yes we have 7 days return and exchange policy


10. Do you have refund policy?

• No, Just Return and Exchange only


11. Do you accept reservations?

• Yes, we have 7 days reservation after placing your order. Beyond 7 days will automatically cancelled.

• Yes. Located at Unit 1-D Ground Floor #80 Maginhawa St., Quezon City (Open from 11:00AM - 11:00PM)
